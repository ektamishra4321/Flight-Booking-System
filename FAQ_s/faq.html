Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)


  • What is online booking ?

    • Online booking through our website is a convenient way of booking your travel over the internet.

  • What are the time restrictions of buying a ticket online?

    Online booking is available for all flights up to 2 hours before scheduled departure time.
    Online booking is available for any flight, with a scheduled departure up to 361 days in the future.

  • Can I make an online booking for a child traveling alone?

    No, you cannot make a booking for a child travelling alone.

  • How can I book a one way or return travel?

    Select the "One way” radio button on the "Search Flights” tab for one-way travel.
    The "Return” radio button is used for booking return point to point travel.

  • Can I purchase an open-dated ticket when I book online?

    No. The selected sectors must be booked on a confirmed basis and paid for at the same time.

  • What flights are available for online bookings on www.aerofly.in ?

    Online booking is available for India operated flights, on all destinations on the Indianetwork.
