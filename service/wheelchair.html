Senior Citizen Services

Opt for wheelchair assistance

AeroFly treats all its customers with the same dignity and respect, especially old timers. Special services like wheelchairs are available at the airport itself, in case it is required. In case of individual travel, a staff member may also assist you in moving around the airport.

Discounts for senior citizens

  • Eligibility: A Senior citizen of Indian Nationality, permanently residing in India and should have attained the age of 60 years on the date of commencement of journey.
  • Required Documents: Any valid Photo ID with date of birth e.g., Voter’s ID card, Passport, Driving license, senior citizens ID card issued by Air India etc .
  • Discount: 50% of Basic fare on select booking classes in Economy cabin. For tickets issued on/after 29th September 2022, 25% of Basic fare will be applicable on select booking classes in Economy cabin.
  • Travel: Any sector within India.
